Visualization of Alec Ankle Recovering from Sprain
Link to Measurement and Step Count Data of Alec's Foot
On September 19, Alec was playing tennis and sprained his ankle. We had to pick him up at Mount Sinani Hospital where the x-ray technicians commented on his ankle, "It looks like the tennis ball went into your foot!"

This visualization looking at the inflammation and swelling of Alec's right foot over the fifteen days and how many step he took. Daily measurement were taken at two points (circumference 1 and 2 shown below) with a tailor's measuring tape. By using the pedometer on his iPhone, there is an additional layer of information showing how much Alec was able to walk and at what hours.
